Old wiring causing concerns? We carry out full and partial rewires to bring your home up to current BS 7671 standards — safely and with minimal disruption.

Consumer Unit (Fuse Board) Upgrades

Landlords, homeowners, and buyers all need an up-to-date EICR. We carry out thorough inspections and provide a clear, certified report.

About us

A Local Electrician You Can Actually Trust

Blake Electrical Contractors is a Porthcawl-based electrical contractor covering Bridgend and the surrounding area. We specialise in domestic work — from quick repairs and fault finding to full rewires, fuse board upgrades, and EV charger installations. Every job gets the same level of care, whether it's a single socket or a whole house.

What makes us different? We turn up when we say we will, we explain everything clearly before we start, and we leave your home tidy.
